About Me

Acts. Sings. Makes Weird Faces.

I'm an experienced theatre actor based in Portsmouth, Hampshire. I specialize in theatrical performances, but have experience in street theatre, improvisation, monologues, and historical re-enactment. I also hold the dubious honour of performing the first play whilst sat on a toilet in the United Kingdom.
