About Me

Acts. Sings. Makes Weird Faces.

I'm an experienced theatre actor based in Portsmouth, Hampshire. I specialize in theatrical performances, and have additional experience in street theatre, improvisation, monologues, and historical re-enactment. I'm always out to expand my skills and try out new methods of acting. I hold the (perhaps dubious!) honour of performing the first play set in a toilet cubicle in the United Kingdom!

I own wigs and hair extensions and can make my own costumes to provide an array of versatile characters, historical and modern.

My current personal career goal is to attend an accredited drama school on a 1-year Masters course, to give myself 24/7 education immersion and challenge my existing abilities. To this end, I am working hard to gain experience across a wealth of genres and to save up the required funds.
